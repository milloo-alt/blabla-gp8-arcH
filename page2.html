2.Kan Thar Yar Lake.

Kayin State

By Renn

Surrounded by limestone mountains, it offers breathtaking views and a peaceful atmosphere. A wooden bridge stretches across the lake, providing a perfect spot for photography and relaxation. The lake is a popular destination for locals and tourists seeking natural beauty and tranquility.

3.Myeik.

Tanintharyi Region

By Renn

Formerly Mergui, is a coastal city in Myanmar's Tanintharyi Region. Known for fishing, rubber, and bird's nest trade, it's the gateway to the Mergui Archipelago, famous for its pristine islands. The city blends cultural heritage with stunning natural beauty.

6.Pathein.

Ayyarwady Region

By Nyi Nyi

Pathein is the largest city and the capital of the Ayeyarwady Region. It is also good place to do business. It is located 190 km west of Yangon. It is famous for hand handeumbrell and famous pagoda. Famous pagoda are Shwemokhtaw Pagoda and Thihoshin Phondawpyi pagoda.

5.Shwe Maw Daw.

Bago Region

By Nyi Nyi

A historical pagoda that is higher than Shwe Dagon Pagoda and spreads religious light. The pagoda has been damaged by natural disasters and violence, but has enjoyed reconstructions. It stands strong to this day and is a reliable historical site for religious beliefs.

4.Saddan Cave.

Kayin State.

By Nyi Nyi

Saddan Cave is Buddhist cave temple with hundreds of small Buddha statues. It is located in the southernmost point of the Zwegabin Mountain range, Hpa-An, Kayin State. It is 107 meters long from east to west and 40 meters wide.
