1. Rih Lake.

Chin State

By Hazel

Rih Lake is shaped like a heart by nature. The scenery is beautiful and surrounded by forests. Far from the city noise, you can feel close to nature. The lake has been formed by nature. The water is clear and cool, attracting tourists.

2.Thanbodhay Pagoda.

Monywa, Saging Region

By Hazel

Pagoda  combines Thai and Indian art styles.  The decorations are unique.  It is mysterious because of the colorful paintingsand artistic creations. It is located near the Salween River and Combined with nature.

3.Shwebon Yadana Mingala.

Shwebo, Saging Region

By Hazel

Palace is one of the palaces of the Myanmar kings.  The ancient statues in the palace have a long history,  The majesty and value of the location can be felt for oneself.   Pilgrimage is possible for those with faith.

6.Nat Ma Taung.

Chin State

By Emoji

Also known as Mount Victoria , also called Nat Ma Taung, the clouds formed by the natural giant look like a sea and are beautiful.  It also attracts tourists because it looks like a sea of ​​clouds.

5.Blue Water Lagoon.

Shan State

By Emoji

Blue Water Lagoon, Ywar Ngan lake is blue by nature. The water is clear and cool and beautiful. You can take beautiful photos because of the blue mist and green trees. It is quiet and peaceful, and you can hear the sound of the environment.

4.Amarapura palace.

Mandalay Region

By Emoji

Palace is a place where the kings of the Gonepaw era lived. It was founded by King Naungmin in 1783. You can visit the temples associated with the palace.  You can also study Burmese religious culture.
